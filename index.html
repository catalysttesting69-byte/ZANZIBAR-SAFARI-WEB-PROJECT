Welcome to Zanzibar Safari

Where Every Journey Becomes a Story

Zanzibar is an archipelago steeped in history and blessed with natural beauty. At Zanzibar Safari, We are locals passionate about sharing the authentic soul of our island.

From the winding alleys of Stone Town to the spice farms and the crystalline waters of Nungwi, we curate experiences that go beyond the guidebooks, ensuring safe, comfortable, and unforgettable journeys.

Curated Island Adventures

Prison Island

Discover Prison Island's rich history and natural beauty. Enjoy crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and ancient Aldabra tortoises on this serene retreat, just a boat ride from Stone Town.

Half Day Adventure

Jozani Forest

Explore Jozani Forest, Zanzibar's only national park. Encounter the rare Red Colobus monkeys in their natural habitat within this lush tropical forest.

3 Hours Nature

Stone Town

Step back in time with a guided tour of Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Wander ancient streets, vibrant markets, and admire richly carved doors.

2 Hours Culture

Kuza Cave

Experience Kuza Cave in Jambiani, a hidden gem for adventurers. Swim in crystal-clear spring water and explore ancient limestone formations. A unique dive into Swahili culture.

2 Hours Adventure

Maalum Cave

Adventure awaits at Maalum Cave in Paje. Swim in crystal-clear turquoise freshwater within a 50-meter sink cave, offering a unique blend of nature and Swahili culture.

2 Hours Culture

Salaam Cave

Swim with sea turtles in Salaam Cave's natural aquarium. Feed these beautiful creatures and capture unforgettable photos and videos of your marine encounter.

2 Hours Marine Life

Dolphin tour-Mnemba Atoll

Experience the best of Mnemba Atoll with a combined dolphin tour and snorkeling adventure. Swim alongside playful dolphins and explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with diverse marine life in pristine waters.

Half Day Marine

Sunset Cruise

A relaxing and romantic cruise for all. Enjoy drinks, fruits, and Zanzibar snacks aboard, accompanied by traditional taarab music or lively African ngoma dance.

2 Hours Romance

Natural Sea Turtles Aquarium

Swim with beautiful sea turtles and diverse marine life in a natural aquarium. Feed the turtles and capture memorable photos and videos of your experience.

2 Hours Wildlife

Sky diving

Take your adventure to new heights in Zanzibar. Jump from 10,000 feet and experience an exhilarating freefall before gliding gently under canopy with panoramic views of the islands.

Half Day Adventure

The Rock Restaurant

Dine uniquely at The Rock Restaurant, perched in the Indian Ocean. Savor fresh seafood like octopus, lobster, and prawns, perfect for couples, families, and friends.

1.5 Hours Dining

Day-trip safari to Mikumi

Embark on an authentic full-day safari to Mikumi National Park. Witness rare wildlife in their natural habitat with an experienced guide, a true Tanzanian wilderness adventure.

Full Day Wildlife

Nakupenda sandbank

Nakupenda (meaning 'I love you') offers relaxation, swimming, and snorkeling near Stone Town. Enjoy a delicious seafood BBQ lunch on the sandbank before sailing back.

Half Day Beach

Safari blue

Embark on an unforgettable full-day Safari Blue adventure. Snorkel vibrant coral reefs, visit secluded sandbanks, and indulge in a seafood BBQ on Kwale Island.

Full Day Adventure

Masingini forest

Explore the wild heart of Zanzibar with a guided walk through Masingini Forest. Discover rare wildlife, panoramic views, and serene trails, just minutes from town.

3 Hours Nature

Spice Tour

Experience the rich aromas and vibrant flavors of Zanzibar on a guided Spice Tour through the island’s lush plantations. Known as the “Spice Island,” Zanzibar offers an immersive journey where you’ll see, smell, and taste a variety of spices.

2 Hours Culture

Swimming with horses

Swimming can be done by any level of rider including beginners, however you must be able to swim. Swimming with horses can only be done at high tide.

1 Hour Adventure

Jet ski

Make unforgettable family memories with a Jet Ski adventure on Zanzibar’s sparkling blue waters. Ride across gentle waves, enjoy stunning coastline views, and share the excitement together.

30 Mins Adventure
